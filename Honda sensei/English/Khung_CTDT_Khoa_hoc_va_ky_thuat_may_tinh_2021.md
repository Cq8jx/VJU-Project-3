### 1. Basic Information about the Program

- **Administering Institution:** Vietnam Japan University, Hanoi National University

- **Note:** A high-quality human resources training program tailored to the specific characteristics of the institution.

### 2. Program Objectives

#### 2.1. General Objectives

The general objective of this program is to train human resources in the field of Computer Science and Engineering who possess a high level of competitiveness in the domestic, Japanese, and global labor markets in the era of the Fourth Industrial Revolution and Society 5.0.

The program aims to provide students with fundamental, modern, and practical knowledge in Computer Science and Engineering, and to equip them with the ability to apply hardware, software, and data analysis techniques based on artificial intelligence to solve problems in information technology and interdisciplinary applications. It also aims to foster the ability to work effectively in practice, demonstrate creativity, and promote sustainable development (harmonizing the goals of economic growth, social progress, and environmental protection).

#### 2.2. Specific Objectives

- **Regarding Knowledge:** To provide students with a broad knowledge base in fundamental sciences, sustainability science, and essential knowledge for humanity in the 21st century, along with deep professional knowledge to solve problems in the field of Computer Science and Engineering.
- **Regarding Skills:** To enhance students' international working and integration skills, utilizing English proficiency equivalent to Level 4 of the Vietnamese 6-level Framework of Foreign Language Proficiency. To improve practical and hands-on skills, and the ability to apply advanced achievements in Computer Science and Engineering to professional practice, especially in solving multidisciplinary and interdisciplinary problems.
- **Regarding Attitude:** To equip students with a proactive, self-directed, creative, and professional attitude in their work and life. In particular, to cultivate a capacity for lifelong learning to meet the new trends of present and future society.

### 3. Admission Information

- In accordance with the university admission regulations of Hanoi National University and the annual university admission plan of Vietnam Japan University approved by Hanoi National University.

#### Note

Students must select specialization-oriented subjects based on the guidance of the Faculty of Advanced Engineering and Technology. Specifically, students specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science should choose CSE3050, CSE3051, CSE3057; those specializing in Software Engineering should choose CSE3052, CSE3053, CSE3056; those specializing in Financial Technology should choose CSE3054, CSE3055, CSE3071, CSE3072; those specializing in Informatics in Engineering should choose CSE3073, CSE3074, CSE3075, CSE3076, or make selections based on consultation with their graduation thesis advisor.

The foreign language courses English B1, English B2, and Japanese A1 are included in the total credits of the program, but the assessment results of these courses are not included in the calculation of the semester GPA, knowledge block GPA, and cumulative GPA.

Students who wish to continue learning Japanese can register for advanced classes according to the Japanese language training program offered by Vietnam Japan University. If the grade for an advanced Japanese course is D or higher, it will be recorded on the certificate of course completion. Students must pay additional tuition fees corresponding to these courses.

Physical Education and National Defense and Security Education are not included in the total credits of the program and are not included in the calculation of the semester GPA, knowledge block GPA, and cumulative GPA, but they are a requirement for graduation.
